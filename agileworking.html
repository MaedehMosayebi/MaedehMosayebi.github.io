PROBLEMIN IN PAYMENT PAGES

Here you're going to read about how to solve a challenge in Agile team work in limited time,with hight performance.

The truth is that most of us are working on agile, and like it or not (I genuinely enjoy working in agile and working with the developers). The two-week screen is hard for any research to have a significant impact and add any value to any stream work that we have in a product company, and that's a truth that we should consider.

Recruiters see your resume and decide about continuing with you or not. An eye-catching resume is a requirement for job searching in any industry, at any level of experience. But the thought of writing a professional resume on your own leaves a lot of job seekers feeling confused and overwhelmed.

Goals

We were developing payment pages; we had a new challenge except for all the problems and challenges! The discount code Was applied to the source prices. It means that if a user wants to use a code on payment and the price had had a discount before (discounts that the staff applied on price from the company's panel) You are just one more tool there, so the question is, "what can we do about it?". we have no time, and there was a concern about the discount code.

Challenges

In this situation, you have to talk to people because that's expected from a designer; you should do research, usability tests, interviews, and develop a solution in two weeks because developers are waiting, and you don't want to be a bottleneck

Solutions

Here I write about the soloution that we took

As a product designer, I started to do some quick crazy-8 sketches. It is a fast sketching exercise that challenges people to sketch eight distinct ideas in eight minutes. The goal is to push beyond your first idea, frequently the least innovative, and generate a wide variety of solutions to your challenge. After doing that four times, I come up with two solutions for adding a discount on other discounts. It has to be readable and useful for users, but I have a problem with how the new design could hold the last discount applied to the price, it makes an opportunity to the users, they don't lose the previous discount because of a mistake.

I've done some crazy-8 for that too. Then I start preparing wireframes and prepare subjects for quick hallway testing. Before that, I checked the possibility of them with the team lead. The main benefit of hallway testing is the minimal effort in recruiting and scheduling, which can take up time. Also, in most cases, you don't need to provide an incentive for the participants, keeping the budget low.

Results

With all these methods that I integrated, I found the solution in 1 day, and then we need to review the aspects and components and follow up with developers because depending on your company. They might be starting development later, not at the moment that you finished.
