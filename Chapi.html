Dreaming up a Brand-New Business Idea

Taaghche's Bold Leap from Digital to Print! Ever wondered how to blend digital and physical books seamlessly? Taaghche, Iran's top ebook platform, did just that. As the world went digital, they embraced printed books and readers. How? By focusing on users, using smart design, and staying ahead. Explore their journey here.

Getting Everyone on the Same Page

Imagine this: you've got one team juggling digital stuff, another team handling physical books, and a third team dealing with logistics. They had to make sure everyone was on board with the plan to blend digital and physical reading seamlessly. It was a bit like getting a group of rowdy kids to play nicely together in the sandbox. But hey, with a bit of teamwork and some strong coffee, they managed to get everyone moving in the same direction.

So, Taaghche was all about digital books. But then, they got this wild idea: let's add real, paper books too! Crazy, right? But they wanted to do it right, so they had to figure out how to dive into this whole new world of print without making a mess of things. Picture this: digital wizards trying to make sense of old-school ink and paper. But hey, they rolled up their sleeves and got to work, because who doesn't love a good challenge?

Result



Approaching the Taaghche expansion project as a daring venture into uncharted territory, we embarked on an 8-month journey of exploration and innovation. Despite facing unexpected challenges, our dedicated efforts yielded significant progress, reshaping the landscape of our business. Here's a glimpse of our achievements:

  • User Requests: 17,000 in Experimental Phase
    • Initially, we received an average of 30 daily requests, soaring to 150 daily by the project's conclusion.
  • Search Success Rate: 52%
    • Despite occasional hurdles, we achieved a commendable success rate in fulfilling user searches.
  • Response Time: Less than 10 Minutes
    • Through continuous refinement, we reduced response time to under 10 minutes for over 90% of requests.

Despite external uncertainties and investor caution, our commitment to data-driven progress and user satisfaction remained steadfast, driving tangible advancements in our venture.
